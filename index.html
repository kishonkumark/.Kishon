About Us

K K Civil Contractor is a leading Bangalore based construction company specializing in delivering top-quality construction services. With our head office located in Chandra Layout, Bangalore, we have successfully completed projects in various sectors, including commercial, residential, institutional, industrial, and healthcare.

At K K Civil Contractor, our commitment to excellence is evident in our workmanship and efficient supervision. We take pride in our continuous efforts to attain the highest standards in construction. Over the years, we have grown into one of the best construction companies in Bangalore, thanks to our dedication and hard work.

Our team of skilled professionals ensures that each project is executed with precision and meets the unique requirements of our clients. We are passionate about delivering construction solutions that stand the test of time, and our diverse portfolio showcases our capabilities across different sectors.

K K Civil Contractor's moral philosophy revolves around trustworthiness and integrity, serving as the causeway to reach new heights. We have implemented appropriate policies and practices for all aspects of business conduct for individuals and the entire organization. Our devotion is evident in our integrity, honesty, and teamwork. We are a truly devoted and committed firm in the field of construction, working with a highly spirited team to accomplish set goals, which has built us a great reputation in Bangalore.
